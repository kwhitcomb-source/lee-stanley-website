Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Lee-Stanley Corporation respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Summary

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not rent your personal information.
  • We use information to operate and improve the site, respond to inquiries, and support business communications.

Information we collect

Information you provide: If you contact us by email, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, organization, and any message content you send.

Information collected automatically: Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect basic log data, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This information is used for security, performance, and reliability.

Cookies and similar technologies: This site may use cookies that are necessary for basic website operation. If we add analytics or marketing tools in the future, we will update this policy.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information
  • To operate, maintain, and secure the website
  • To improve content, performance, and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations when required

How we share information

We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website and business communications. These providers are permitted to use information only to perform services for us.

SMS Messaging Privacy

Anamnesis is a service of Lee-Stanley, Corp. If you opt in to receive SMS messages from Anamnesis, we will use your mobile number only to send appointment reminders, appointment confirmations, and service-related notifications that you requested. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except with vendors, aggregators, and service providers that help us deliver our text messages and support our messaging services.

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at info@lee-stanley.com or 844-314-3274.

We may also share information if required by law, to protect our rights, or to prevent fraud or security issues.

Third party links

This site includes links to third party websites, such as PMO Depot®. If you follow those links, you are subject to the privacy practices of those sites.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to respond to you, provide services, maintain records, or meet legal requirements.

Your choices

  • You can contact us to request updates or deletion of information you provided, where applicable.
  • You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
